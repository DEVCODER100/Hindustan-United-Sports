— About the Academy —

Built on passion. Trained with precision.

Hindustan United Sports is a Vadodara-based football coaching academy dedicated to developing complete footballers. We combine modern training methodologies, licensed coaching, and four professional grounds to give every player the platform they deserve.

Licensed Coaching

Train under AIFF C & D-licensed football coaches with structured, age-appropriate curriculums.

Player Development

From basic technique to elite tactical awareness — every session is purposeful and measurable.

Discipline & Values

We build athletes and people. Punctuality, teamwork, respect and sportsmanship come first.

Performance Focus

Strength, stamina, agility and football-specific conditioning baked into every program.
